The Evolution of IoT

IoT began as a concept of connected devices and has evolved into a transformative technology. From early RFID tags to today's smart cities, IoT has revolutionized industries and daily life.

Future Potential

IoT is poised to play a pivotal role in healthcare, transportation, and sustainability. With advancements in AI and 5G, the possibilities are endless.

  • Healthcare: Remote patient monitoring and predictive diagnostics.
  • Transportation: Autonomous vehicles and smart traffic systems.
  • Sustainability: Energy-efficient systems and waste reduction.
